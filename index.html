Problem Statement

Gen-Z struggles with personal growth & learning consistency in today's fast-paced digital world.

Social platforms are designed to be distracting (likes, followers) leading to low retention of meaningful content.

Existing learning apps are static, non-adaptive, and not engaging enough for the modern user.

Young adults need guidance but often lack access to personalized mentoring and growth resources.

Solution – SoulCircle

Daily AI-generated video tasks personalized to the user's face, voice, and emotions.

Emotion-based feedback loop where AI measures engagement & progress for continuous improvement.

Community without followers/likes that connects people based on shared growth goals rather than popularity.

AI knowledge updates on chosen topics & trends to keep content relevant and engaging.

Gamified learning experience with rewards and milestones to maintain user motivation.
